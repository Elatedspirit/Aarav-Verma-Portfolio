About My Journey

Aarav Verma is a dedicated professional with a strong foundation in computer science and artificial intelligence. He holds a Diploma in Computer Science from ACMT Group of Colleges, affiliated with Arunachal University of Studies, and is currently pursuing a BTech in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at Inderprastha Engineering College, under Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam Technical University. His educational background is further complemented by his 10th grade education from Vivekanand School, affiliated with CBSE.


Aarav possesses a diverse skill set, including proficiency in programming languages such as C and C++, web development technologies like HTML5, Bootstrap, CSS, and WordPress, and various digital marketing tools. His expertise extends to videography, photography, and content creation using Adobe Premier Pro and Photoshop. Aarav is also knowledgeable in SEO, Google Ad making, and product management on platforms like WIX, Shopify, and Canva.


His career objective is to secure a responsible professional opportunity that allows him to leverage his education and experience while contributing to the organization's growth. Aarav has demonstrated his skills in competitive projects, such as participating in poster making at Inderprastha Engineering College's Eureka International Conference, where he achieved 41st position. He also participated in THINK AI CLUB's web development competition at IPEC, showcasing his skills in web development, UX/UI design, and collaboration.


Aarav's professional experience includes working as a frontend developer intern at Myitronline and as the Head of Indian Operations at TDG Data Concept Inc. He has handled various responsibilities, from WordPress E-commerce website development and digital marketing to SEO and hosting. His experience with ex-clients like Spine care, TDG Data Concept Inc., Jyoti Grocery, and others highlights his ability to deliver comprehensive digital solutions. Aarav's strengths include being hard-working, a quick learner, possessing good communication skills, and being a self-motivator with a positive attitude. He is patient and enjoys hobbies such as painting, learning new things about apps, and listening to music. Aarav's father, Late Rajender Kumar, has been a significant influence in his life, and Aarav continues to strive for excellence in his professional journey.
